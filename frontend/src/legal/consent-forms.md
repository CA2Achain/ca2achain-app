## Buyer Registration Consent Form

### California AB 1263 Firearm Accessory Verification Disclosure

**IMPORTANT: Please read this disclosure carefully before proceeding with identity verification.**

#### Legal Requirement Notice

California Assembly Bill 1263, effective 2026, requires online sellers of firearm accessories to verify the age and identity of purchasers before completing sales. This law aims to prevent firearm accessories from reaching prohibited persons.

#### What This Means for You

**Identity Verification Required:**
- You must provide government-issued photo identification
- Your age and California residency will be verified
- This is a one-time $39 process for lifetime verification

**Information Collection and Sharing:**
- We will collect and verify your personal information through Persona
- Your verified identity will be stored securely and encrypted
- When you make purchases, dealers will receive only "verified" or "not verified" status
- Dealers will NOT receive your personal information (name, address, date of birth, ID number)

**Privacy Protection Through Zero-Knowledge Technology:**
- Your personal data is encrypted and protected
- Dealers receive mathematical proof of verification without seeing your actual information
- Verification records are stored on blockchain for legal compliance (no personal data)
